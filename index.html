About The Ballers

We have created a computer generated collection of pixellated ballers that earn a share of the sales proceeds of the collection. Each baller belongs to one of the 32 teams participation in 2022 World Cup. Your baller earns when they player they are paired with scores or when their team wins a match, a group stage or the World Cup itself!

How to Play?

Its easy to play, you just buy an NFT and wait for your earnings to be sent

Each baller is guaranteed to be unique, and will belong to one of the 32 teams that play in the World Cup this year. The baller will also have a number and a position. Each time the team your baller is on wins a match in the World Cup, your baller earns a salary paid from the NFT collection sales proceeds (10% of every baller sale during the World Cup goes into the prize pool).

For example, If you have 3 ballers who belong to the German team and Germany wins a match, each ballers gets a bonus. If you collect and entire qualifying starting lineup (one baller for every position), your bonus for each baller is doubled. Also, if your baller number matches the jersey number of an actual player playing in the match for the team its on, then if that number scores a goal, you also get some bonus money. And if your team wins the World Cup, there is an extra bonus for each baller you have on that winning team.

If you download the Android app (ballers World Cup Edition by TFA), there are more prizes available in addition. (Predictions games, play to earn games unlocked daily by your NFT)

Special bonus

If you manage to collect a baller for every position on a team (11 positions), you receive extra earnings. We will publish the qualifying lineups, and our computer system automatically knows if you are qualified and will notify you in the app!
